Global Community

Our members have a long-standing passion for international cultures. Many of our athletes are actively involved with the International Exchange Center, making it the perfect environment for cross-cultural communication.

Social Events

Beyond the track, we host welcome parties and "Talk & SNS" sessions. It's a great way to build strong ties with Japanese students who are eager to learn about your home country!
